## Key Metrics Explained

### Total Hours
- **What:** Sum of hours tutored (completed sessions)
- **Shows:** How much you've taught
- **Used for:** Portfolio building, credibility

### Total Earnings
- **What:** Total income from completed sessions
- **Formula:** Completed Sessions Ã— $10
- **Shows:** Revenue generated
- **Used for:** Income tracking, motivation

### Average Rating
- **What:** Mean of all student ratings (1-5 stars)
- **Shows:** Quality feedback from students
- **Range:** 0.0 - 5.0
- **Used for:** Tutor credibility, filtering (if platform uses it)

### Pending Requests
- **What:** Number of unresponded booking requests
- **Shows:** Immediate action items
- **Action:** Accept or Decline in the sidebar
- **Used for:** Time management

---

## Chart Descriptions

### Weekly Activity (Bar Chart)
**What:** Number of sessions booked per day (last 7 days)
**Benefits:**
- See your busiest days
- Plan calendar accordingly
- Identify slow periods

**Example Reading:**
- Monday: 2 sessions
- Tuesday: 4 sessions (busiest)
- Friday: 1 session (slowest)

---

### Subject Popularity (Doughnut Chart)
**What:** Distribution of bookings by subject (top 5)
**Benefits:**
- Identify most in-demand subjects
- Plan marketing strategy
- Allocate time to specialties

**Example Reading:**
- Mathematics: 35% of bookings (most popular)
- English: 25%
- Science: 20%
- History: 15%
- Art: 5% (least popular)

---

### Monthly Trend (Line Chart)
**What:** Sessions booked per month (last 6 months)
**Benefits:**
- Visualize growth trajectory
- Predict future demand
- Celebrate milestones

**Example Reading:**
- June: 8 sessions (starting point)
- July: 12 sessions (50% growth)
- August: 14 sessions (peak)
- September: 10 sessions (drop - summer break?)
- October: 16 sessions (recovery)
- November: 18 sessions (continuing growth)

## What Each Card Tells You

### Upcoming Sessions
"I have X sessions coming up. Here's when and with whom."
- **Action:** Prepare materials, confirm with students
- **Benefits:** Don't forget bookings, stay organized

### Booking Requests
"X students want to book with me. I need to respond."
- **Action:** Accept good requests, decline if fully booked
- **Benefits:** Quick decision-making, fast response times

### Recent Feedback
"Here's what my students think."
- **Action:** Learn from comments, improve weak areas
- **Benefits:** Build credibility, track improvement

### Performance Metrics
"How well am I doing overall?"
- **Completion Rate:** Are students completing sessions?
- **Acceptance Rate:** Am I rejecting too many requests?

### Specializations
"Here's what I teach."
- **Action:** Ensure subjects are up-to-date
- **Benefits:** Students can quickly identify relevant tutor

## How to Interpret the Dashboard

### Day 1 (New Tutor)
- All metrics are 0
- No charts (no data yet)
- Use quick actions to set availability
- Wait for first booking

### Day 7 (Early Growth)
- 2-3 hours tutored
- $20-30 in earnings
- 1-2 ratings appearing
- 1-2 pending requests
- Weekly chart shows activity pattern

- Monthly chart shows growth trend
- Clear subject popularity pattern

## Tips for Success

1. **Monitor Pending Requests**
   - Respond within 24 hours
   - High acceptance rate = more bookings

2. **Watch Your Acceptance Rate**
   - Aim for 80%+
   - Only decline if truly unavailable

3. **Improve Your Rating**
   - Good feedback starts with quality teaching
   - Respond to comments
   - Adjust based on feedback

4. **Grow Your Peak Subject**
   - If Math is 40% of bookings, specialize there
   - Market that strength

5. **Smooth Out Slow Days**
   - Add availability on slow days
   - Offer discounts during slow periods
   - Promote underutilized time slots

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why is my rating "N/A"?**
A: You haven't received any student feedback yet. After your first completed session, you'll get your first rating.

**Q: How is earnings calculated?**
A: Currently $10 per completed session. Admin can adjust this globally.

**Q: Why are pending requests shown on dashboard?**
A: Fast response time is critical. Dashboard surfacing them encourages quick decisions.

**Q: Can I see all feedback, not just recent 3?**
A: Yes, go to `/tutor/feedback` (Menu â†’ Manage Feedback)

**Q: What does "Completion Rate" mean?**
A: % of accepted sessions that were actually completed (not cancelled). Aim for 90%+.

**Q: How do I improve my acceptance rate?**
A: Availability management. Add more time slots so you can accept more requests.
