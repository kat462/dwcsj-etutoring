## 1. Tutor Profile & Public Profile

## 2. Browse Tutors & Search/Filter
**Example:**
- Tutee Maria browses tutors by subject (e.g., Math).
- She uses the search bar to filter tutors by name or subject.
- She clicks "View Availability" to see open slots for a tutor.

---

## 3. Booking a Session
**Example:**
- Maria selects a date/time from Juan's available slots and clicks "Book".
- She adds a note (optional) and confirms the booking.
- Juan receives a booking request notification.

---

## 4. Payment Flow
**Example:**
- Maria chooses a payment method (e.g., GCash, PayMaya, Cash) when booking a paid session.
- She enters a reference number if required.
- Payment status is shown in her bookings list.

---

## 5. Calendar View
**Example:**
- Maria opens her calendar to see all upcoming and past sessions.
- Juan views his own calendar to manage availabilities and bookings.

---

## 6. Feedback & Ratings
**Example:**
- After a completed session, Maria clicks "Leave Feedback" for Juan.
- She gives a 5-star rating and a comment: "Very helpful and patient!"
- Juan's average rating updates and is visible on his profile.

---

## 7. Admin Dashboard
**Example:**
- Admin sees total users, bookings, feedback, and platform analytics.
- Admin can moderate feedback and manage users.

---

## 8. Profile Photo Upload
**Example:**
- Juan uploads a new profile photo from the Edit Profile page.
- The new photo is instantly shown on his profile and public page.

## 9. Notifications
